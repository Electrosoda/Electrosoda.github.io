About Me

As a UX designer with two years of experience, I've worked on various projects, from million-user products to local businesses. Before this, I taught fashion management and design at the UK and China's leading universities. This background has given me a strong foundation in theory and practice, which I have brought to my work later in UX design.

My experience in management research has helped me develop a strong business view and market research skills, which I use to design products that meet user needs and business objectives. I'm comfortable using research to guide my design choices and create competitive products.

My experience in fashion design has honed my visual and aesthetic sensibilities, as well as my software skills, allowing me to create visually appealing designs that are also functional and user-friendly.

My diverse background and skill set could be a valuable asset to various design and business roles, particularly those that require a balance of creative and analytical thinking.

Skills

  • Proficiency with UX tools such as Figma, Sketch, Adobe XD, but also design tools like Photoshop, Adobe Illustration
  • Familiarity with user research methods such as surveys, interviews, and usability testing
  • Ability to create wireframes, prototypes, and high-fidelity designs
  • Understanding of information architecture and user flows
